Admissions

Join one of the top engineering institutions and start your journey to success.

Undergraduate Admissions

Admission to undergraduate programs is based on entrance exam scores and merit.

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with PCM (Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics)
  • Entrance Exam: JEE Main or State Engineering Entrance

Postgraduate Admissions

Postgraduate programs are open to applicants with a relevant bachelor's degree.

  • Eligibility: Relevant undergraduate degree
  • Entrance Exam: GATE or equivalent exam
